Let's use shadows again - they do not extend the viewport

Shadows, at least on Chrome, are limited to 200px - but that's enough for us

            .tab-header {
                width: ~"calc((100vw - 120%) / 2 + 100% - 50px)";
                box-shadow: 100px 0 0 0 yellow;
            }

Now our header extends to the edge of the screen - without extra scrollbars
