Why this presentation?

  • It may ease your suffering
  • You don’t always have the full control over all components of your program:
    • Some 3rd party tools won’t give JS reliable access to internal events
    • But many 3rd party tools will assign CSS classes to modified elements
  • JS layout changes may be unreliable
  • If you want to produce good UI, simple “border: 1px solid” won’t be enough
  • Many features that are missing in CSS. But sometimes you can get around them
  • Stockholm Syndrome?

And maybe this presentation will help.
